Echoes of Glory

Historical Monuments & Museums

Discover the valiant tales of Indian independence fighters, captivating heritage ruins, and rich anthropological evolution.

National Memorial

Cellular Jail (Kala Pani)

Port Blair

The monumental three-story colonial prison that once held prominent political exiles. Experience the highly emotional, patriotically uplifting Sound and Light evening show narrating rich history.

Dedicated to the brave unyielding spirits of legendary freedom fighters who sacrificed their lives.
Colonial Architecture

Ross Island Heritage Ruins

Near Port Blair

Once known as the 'Paris of the East' serving as the luxurious British administrative headquarters. Today, majestic structures like the old church, ballroom, and bakery are beautifully reclaimed by nature.

Gallows & History

Viper Island

Port Blair Harbor

Housing the hilltop remains of the old gallows where prisoners were kept before the Cellular Jail was built. Offers a solemn, tranquil boat excursion past scenic harbor vistas.

Tribal Heritage

Anthropological Museum

Port Blair

An exquisitely curated collection providing profound insights into the native Paleolithic tribal communities of the islands including Jarawas, Onges, Sentinelese, and Shompens.

Marine Conservation

Samudrika Naval Marine Museum

Delanipur, Port Blair

Maintained directly by the Indian Navy to generate awareness regarding ocean ecology. Showcases impressive rare sets of colorful sea shells, tribal weaponry, and local ecosystem dioramas.
