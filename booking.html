Everything you need to know about traveling to the Andaman & Nicobar archipelago safely and luxuriously.

Indian citizens do not require any restricted area permits to visit Port Blair, Havelock, or Neil Island. Foreign nationals are issued Restricted Area Permits (RAP) free of charge directly upon arrival at Veer Savarkar Airport, granting authorization for up to 30 days.

The premium touring peak stretches from October to May. During these months, the waters are glassy, flat, and feature peak underwater visibility for scuba diving. Monsoons (June to September) offer dramatic tropical rains but trigger choppy transit seas.

Since the activation of the submarine optical fiber cable from Chennai, mobile speeds have significantly improved. Airtel, Jio, and BSNL 4G run reliably across Port Blair and Havelock. Premium resort partners offer complementary high-speed Wi-Fi linkages.
